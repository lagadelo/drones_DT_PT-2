8000 drones | 50       | -0.006% per       | Fast auto-recovery
10000       | 60       | -0.006% per       | Immediate stabilization

**Strategic insight**: Massive fleets are inherently resilient to component

Individual failures are absorbed by the ensemble without service degradation.
